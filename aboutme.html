A little about me...

Hi, I’m Steven Cabrera, a graphic designer and photographer with a passion for exploring diverse styles and media. My work ranges from minimal to abstract, often inspired by a blend of artistic influences—from painters and designers to music artists. Through my adaptable approach, I strive to create visuals that are uniquely expressive and capture emotion through storytelling.

My inspiration comes from exploring the work of artists across all types of media—whether they’re painters, designers, or musicians. I spend time immersing myself in these different styles, gathering ideas from each. From there, I like to head outdoors or let the ideas settle until I’m ready to create a unique, personal take on that inspiration. This process helps me bring a fresh perspective to each project, aiming to convey emotion and tell a story through my art.

When I'm not designing or photographing, I’m likely playing video games or spending time with my dog, Pao Pao. Exploring nature and discovering new art forms—whether classic paintings or modern music—keeps my creativity energized and inspired.

Portfolio

Welcome to my showcase of projects I’ve worked on in the past. I have compiled a variety of projects design and photography.

Postcard

This project features a vintage-inspired postcard design capturing the charm of Barcelona. As my first project, I wanted to convey the city’s unique atmosphere by focusing on a classic carousel, evoking a sense of nostalgia. The muted colors and retro aesthetic bring out a timeless quality, inviting viewers to experience the whimsical side of Barcelona’s vibrant culture.

Logo work

This section showcases the current evolution of a logo I designed for 'Vindyl,' a personal brand name I’ve cherished since I was young. Over time, the logo has transformed, reflecting my growth and refinement as a designer. Vindyl represents a vision I aim to bring to life, blending personal significance with a clean, versatile design.

Illustrations

This collection highlights my playful, soft-toned illustrations, including a whimsical space-themed sticker set. With a focus on simplicity and gentle colors, each piece brings a lighthearted and imaginative feel. These illustrations capture my exploration of different themes and showcase my evolving style as I continue to develop my skills.

Photography

This section of my portfolio consists of a variety of different photography projects I have done in the past. These range from portrait works, still life, studio, and pet photography.
